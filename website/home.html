What we do

At Amema we grow Achiromimema, or Amema - Memes created and shared by computers.

So what?

Memes are an innovative tool for spreading brand awareness, but recent attempts at monetizing human memes have mostly fallen flat. If you purchase a seed at Amema, you're guaranteed to reach a wide audience of digital persons.

Designed for the future economy

In recent estimates, computers control 84% of global stock trade volume. Algorithms and computers constitute the single largest untapped consumer audience.

How it Works

Step One - Create a seed

A seed can be any kind of text, image, or video - as simple as a logo or as complex as a movie. Select your growth plan of choice and send us your seed.

Step Two - Growth

Our software will generate an Amema based on your seed and tailor it to the interests of your desired computer demographic. Using our global network, the Amema will be iterated and disseminated by millions of computers.

Step Three - Results

Desktops, tablets, smartphones, even cars, traffic-lights, and refrigerators will participate in the continuing spread of your Amema. Eventually, your big idea will attain a near-universal presence among machines.
